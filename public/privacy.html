Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-05

Summary

ReLoved collects the minimum information needed to operate the service (accounts, listings, and contact messages). We do not sell personal data.

Data We Collect

  • Account: email, display name, and UID.
  • Content: listings (title, description, photo, approximate location).
  • Usage: basic analytics events to improve the product.

How We Use Data

  • Authentication and access to the app.
  • Publishing and discovery of items.
  • User-to-user contact about an item.
  • Product improvement (aggregated analytics).

Data Sharing

  • The sender’s email is shared with the item owner when contacting.
  • Infrastructure providers: Firebase and SendGrid.

Retention

See the Data Retention policy referenced in the app.

Account Deletion

You can request account deletion from the app (Profile → Account deletion).

Changes

We may update this policy; we will notify you in-app if required.

This is an MVP policy draft and should be reviewed by legal counsel before App Store submission.
